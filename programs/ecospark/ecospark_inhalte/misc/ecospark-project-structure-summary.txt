================================================================================
ONLINE STORE DESCRIPTION
================================================================================

EcoSpark is a modern, production-ready e-commerce web application specializing in
sustainable, eco-friendly products. The store offers a carefully curated selection
of 12 products across four main categories: Zero Waste Essentials, Grow Green Kits,
Eco Gadgets, and Upcycling Products.

STORE CONCEPT:
--------------
EcoSpark positions itself as "Kleine Dinge, großer Impact" (Small Things, Big Impact),
focusing on small, affordable sustainable products that make a meaningful difference
in everyday life. The brand emphasizes natural minimalism, eco-premium aesthetics,
and genuine sustainability over greenwashing.

PRODUCT CATALOG:
---------------
The store features 12 carefully selected products:

Zero Waste Essentials (4 products):
- Bienenwachstücher Set (€12.90) - Reusable beeswax wraps in 3 sizes

USER EXPERIENCE:
---------------
The store provides a complete shopping experience:

1. Browsing: Users can browse all products, filter by category, or explore
   featured products on the homepage.

2. Product Discovery: Each product has detailed information including:
   - Short and long descriptions
   - Sustainability information
   - Usage instructions
   - Material lists
   - Highlights and benefits
   - Cross-selling suggestions

3. Shopping Cart: Full cart functionality with:
   - Add/remove items
   - Quantity management
   - Price calculation
   - LocalStorage persistence
   - Cart icon with item count in header

4. Checkout: Demo checkout flow with:
   - Customer information form
   - Order summary
   - Confirmation page
   - Ready for payment provider integration (Stripe, Shopify, etc.)

5. Content Pages: Comprehensive information pages:
   - About EcoSpark (mission and values)
   - Sustainability (product selection criteria, packaging, dropshipping)
   - FAQ (accordion-style questions)
   - Shipping & Returns (policies including "Keep it" policy)
   - Contact (contact form)
   - Blog (3 sample posts about sustainability)

DESIGN SYSTEM:
-------------
The store uses a custom design system with eco-friendly colors:

Primary Colors:
- Deep Forest (#1D3925) - Headers, primary text, buttons
- Leaf Green (#94C973) - Accents, primary buttons
- Olive (#7D9A58) - Secondary accents
- Soft Mint (#CDECC5) - Backgrounds, hover states

Neutral Colors:
- Off-White (#F8F7F3) - Main background
- Graphite (#373737) - Body text
- Gray-Low (#D9D9D9) - Borders
- Gray-Mid (#B4B4B4) - Secondary text

Typography:
- Headings: Montserrat (geometric sans-serif)
- Body: Inter (clean sans-serif)
- Badges: Uppercase with increased letter-spacing

Design Principles:
- Minimalist and clean
- Generous whitespace
- Subtle shadows and borders
- Eco-premium aesthetic
- Mobile-first responsive design
